About Me

7th Day of December 1989

 My name is James Lemmen. I am a 34 year old male. Born and raised in Michigan. Currently I live in California, after having spent 4 years in Florida. I recently obtained a certificate for Web Development with Bootstrap through Udemy.

 As you can see by my portfolio I have ample skills nessecary to complete any tasks given to me. These skills include HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript Bootstrap, React, and more.

 As a web developer it is my dream to create websites that help people, such as those with mental disabilities, small business owners, and more. I grew up struggling to find myself and eventually through all the anger, depression, self hatred, I found myself through coding/progrramming.

 I have spent many years learning all I can on my own, starting with my GED. After having entered a program with an estimated 2 year completion time. I finished with a total 56 hour completion time. For someone who couldn't focus on anything once I found my will to go on, I knew there was more left for me to do.

 My greatest acomplishment in life was when I became a real life hero back in highschool. I witnessed a teacher wrongfully accused of misconduct all because he wouldn't fall for a students advances. After proving his innocence and doing the right thing, I was treated with all kinds of disrespect by both students and staff, however, I would do it again.

 In the great words of Collosus and Deadpool "There are only 4 or 5 moments where you can be a hero.", I found one of them. Now I am on a quest to find the others. I believe that using the skills I got that saved my life I will be able to be a hero again and save others or improve their lives too.
